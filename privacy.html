1. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tubify mobile application for Android (the “app”) and to this website (the “site”), together referred to as the “Service.” It describes the types of information the Service does and does not process, how any such information is handled, the legal bases on which we rely where applicable, and the rights and choices available to you.

By downloading, installing, or using Tubify, or by visiting this website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with it, please do not use the Service.

2. The short version

For people who don't want to read every line, here is the essence of how Tubify treats your privacy:

  • Tubify is completely free and contains no advertising.
  • There are no accounts — you never sign up, log in, or provide personal details.
  • We do not collect personal information about you, and we never sell, rent, or trade data.
  • Your downloads, history, and settings stay on your device.
  • The app uses Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, and Remote Config) for anonymous, aggregate usage statistics, crash diagnostics, and configuration — never tied to your identity.

The rest of this document explains each of these points in detail.

3. Information we do not collect

Tubify is designed to function without knowing who you are. Specifically, the Service does not ask for, require, or collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • Account credentials, usernames, or passwords — Tubify has no login system of any kind.
  • Payment or billing information — the app is free and processes no payments.
  • Precise location data such as GPS coordinates.
  • Your contacts, call logs, messages, photos, or other personal files on your device.
  • The specific titles or contents of the videos and audio you choose to download, in any form linked to you.

Because we do not collect this information, we cannot use it, share it, or lose it.

4. Information collected automatically (analytics & diagnostics)

To keep the app stable and understand broadly how it is used, Tubify includes two Google Firebase components: Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These collect limited, anonymous, aggregate information. They do not identify you personally.

Firebase Analytics

Analytics records non-identifying, aggregate usage signals such as the number of app opens, general feature usage, app version, language, country (derived from IP address, which is not stored by us), device model, and operating-system version. This helps us understand which parts of the app are useful and where problems may exist. It is associated with a randomly generated, resettable app-instance identifier, not with your real-world identity.

Firebase Crashlytics

If the app crashes, Crashlytics collects a diagnostic report so we can find and fix the bug. A report may include the type of error, the line of code involved, the app version, the device model, the operating-system version, and the device's state at the time of the crash (such as available memory). These reports are technical in nature and are not used to identify you.

Crash reporting is enabled for released versions of the app only.

5. App configuration (Firebase Remote Config)

Tubify uses Firebase Remote Config to receive small settings from us — for example, the recommended download-engine update channel — so the app can keep working without you having to install an update. When the app fetches this configuration, the Firebase service may receive a randomly generated installation identifier and basic device information needed to deliver the correct settings. This identifier is not tied to your identity, and the configuration we send is the same for all users (or broad groups of users); it is not personalized to you.

6. Data stored only on your device

Everything you actually do in Tubify is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Downloaded files — saved to the folder you choose (by default, your device's Download/Tubify folder).
  • Download history and queue — kept in a local database on the device.
  • App settings — such as your preferred quality, save location, theme, and concurrent-download count.
  • Recycle bin — deleted downloads are held locally for a limited period so you can restore them, then removed.

This information remains under your control. Uninstalling the app, clearing its data, or deleting files removes it from your device. We have no copy of it.

7. Clipboard access

As a convenience, Tubify can detect when you have copied a web link so it can offer to download it with a single tap. Your clipboard is read only on your device, only while the app is open and in focus, and only to check whether the copied text looks like a link. The clipboard contents are not stored and not transmitted anywhere. You are never required to use this feature.

8. Permissions and why we ask for them

Tubify requests only the permissions it needs to function. Each is used for a specific, transparent purpose:

  • Internet access — to fetch media you ask to download and to retrieve the diagnostic and configuration services described above.
  • Storage — to save your downloads to the folder you choose. On modern Android versions, files are written through the system's media framework.
  • Notifications — to show download progress and completion. You can disable these in your system settings at any time.
  • Foreground service — to keep a download (or playback) running reliably while the app is in the background.
  • Install unknown apps (only if you choose in-app updating) — to let you install a newer version of Tubify that you downloaded.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or other sensitive device features.

9. Third-party websites you download from

Tubify lets you download media from many third-party websites and services (for example, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and X). When you download from, or browse, such a site, your device connects directly to that site. That site may collect information from you according to its own privacy policy and terms of service, over which we have no control and for which we are not responsible.

You are responsible for ensuring that your use of downloaded content complies with the rights of the content owners and with the terms of the sites you use. Tubify is a general-purpose tool; please download only content that you own or have permission to save.

10. This website

This website is a simple, static information and download page. It does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or its own analytics. It does not ask you to log in or submit any personal information.

The site loads the Poppins font from Google's font service and is hosted on a static hosting provider; like any website, the servers delivering these resources may automatically log standard technical request data (such as your IP address and browser type) for security and operational purposes, governed by those providers' own policies. The app download itself is served from a third-party file-hosting service (such as GitHub Releases), subject to that provider's policy.

11. How information is used

The limited, anonymous information described above is used solely to:

  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs.
  • Understand, in aggregate, how the app is used so we can improve it.
  • Deliver configuration that keeps the app working over time.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the Service.

We do not use any information for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making that has legal or similarly significant effects on you.

12. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

Where the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, our processing of the limited technical data described in this policy relies on our legitimate interests — namely, keeping the app stable, secure, and functional, and understanding aggregate usage to improve it. This processing is minimal, anonymous in nature, and does not override your rights and freedoms. Where required by law, processing is instead based on your consent, which you may withdraw at any time (see “Limiting analytics” below).

13. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade any information, and we do not share information with third parties for their own marketing. The only third parties involved are the service providers that make the app work — principally Google (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Remote Config) acting as our data processor, and the providers that host this website and the app file.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or if necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others — though, given how little information exists, such disclosure would necessarily be limited.

14. Data retention

Information stored on your device is retained until you delete it, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app. Aggregate analytics and crash data held by Firebase are retained according to Google's standard retention periods for those services. Because this data is not linked to your identity, it cannot be used to single you out after collection.

15. Data security

Network connections made by the app and the site use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) where supported by the destination. Because Tubify holds no accounts and stores your activity only on your own device, the most important protections for your data are the security features of your device itself — such as a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date. No method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, but the minimal, anonymous nature of any data involved substantially limits risk.

16. International data transfers

The diagnostic and configuration services we use are operated by Google and may process data on servers located in countries other than your own, including the United States. Where such transfers occur, the relevant providers implement safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) intended to protect the information in accordance with applicable law.

17. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU/UK) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, the right to data portability, and the right to object to certain processing.

Because Tubify does not collect personal information that identifies you, in most cases there is no personal data for us to retrieve, correct, or delete. You can nonetheless exercise meaningful control directly:

  • Delete on-device data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling it.
  • Reset the anonymous analytics identifier by clearing app data or reinstalling.
  • Limit advertising-related identifiers through your device's system settings.

If you believe we hold any information relating to you and wish to exercise a right, you may contact us (see below). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

18. Limiting analytics and crash reporting

You can reduce or remove the limited diagnostic data described in this policy at any time by:

  • Adjusting your device's privacy and advertising settings (for example, opting out of ad personalization or resetting the advertising ID).
  • Clearing the app's data, which resets its anonymous identifiers.
  • Uninstalling the app, which stops all collection entirely.

None of these steps affect your ability to use the core download features of Tubify.

19. Children's privacy

Tubify is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps. As noted throughout this policy, the app does not collect personal information from any user.

20. Do Not Track

This website does not track you across other websites and does not respond to browser “Do Not Track” signals, because there is no cross-site tracking to disable. The app does not perform cross-app or cross-site behavioral tracking.

21. Third-party links and content

The Service may contain links to, or let you access, third-party websites and content. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through Tubify.

When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page and, where the changes are significant, may provide a more prominent notice.
